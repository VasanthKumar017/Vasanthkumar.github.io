   Smart Contract for Corban Trading:

  • The project aims to develop a blockchain-based carbon credit ecosystem to address issues in traditional carbon markets, such as fragmented implementation, over-crediting, double-spending, and high transaction costs. Key features include a decentralized ledger for transparency and immutability, and smart contracts for efficient, automated transactions. The ecosystem unites stakeholders like project verifiers, the energy industry, NGOs, governments, and citizens, creating a reliable marketplace for carbon credit trading. This initiative promotes transparency, accessibility, and standardization, aligning with global climate efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and encourage sustainable practices.
